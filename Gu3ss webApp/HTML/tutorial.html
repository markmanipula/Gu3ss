Gu3ss

How to play: figure out the different codes (0~9) and their position generated by game system.


Input your three digit code here:


Note that the first number cannot start at 0 and there cant be any repeating numbers.

Depending on your guess, there will be a corresponding output. An output of 'A' means you got the
number in the right position. 'B' means you got the correct number but in the wrong position.

For example:
1A 0B (this means you got one of the number correctly in the right position)
1A 1B (this means you got two numbers correctly, but only one is in the right position)
